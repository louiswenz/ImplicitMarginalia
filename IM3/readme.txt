## Implicit Marginalia
# Introduction
Implicit marginalia refers to annotations, comments, or interpretations that can be inferred from a text, whether it's a literary work, historical document, or any form of written expression. These marginalia offer insights into the thoughts, emotions, and perspectives of the author or reader.
